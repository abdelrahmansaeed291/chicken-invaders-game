● The environment :
○ The player is positioned in the middle of the screen at the start of the game,
○ Enemy should be at the top half of the screen and has a size bigger than the player,
○ Health bar displayed on screen for the enemy,
○ 3 lives displayed on screen for the player.
● The player: The player starts at a fixed position in the middle of the screen and is
controlled by the keyboard keys to move in 4 directions.
● The enemy: Starts at the top of the screen and then moves a continuous motion from left
to right or from right to left of the screen, if the enemy moves to the right then it should
reach the border of the screen then change direction to move to the left and vice versa,
● The power-ups:
○ You are free to choose the color of the player, however, the functionality of the power
up is to change the players’ color to any other color to indicate that the player has
acquired a power up and if the player was to collide with one or more of the bullets
from the enemy no lives will be lost,
○ Power-ups always appear randomly at various positions and remain fixed in position
until acquired by the player, in this case the power up should then disappear,
○ The effect of the power up should last for a few seconds then disappear,
○ The player should be able to miss the power up, for example, if the player passes next
to a power up but does not collide with it, the player does not acquire the power up.
● Player Lives: Whenever the player collides with one of the bullets, they should lose one of
the 3 lives in total. You are free to choose between a health bar or simply a number to
indicate the number of lives the player has. Whichever you choose, has to be shown on
screen.
